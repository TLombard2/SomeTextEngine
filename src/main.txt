var money 0
color green
text You find yourself in a bustling village.
text There is a MARKET where people are selling their goods, a FORTUNE teller giving out free palm readings, and a FOUNTAIN in the middle of the square.
input
if input market
    goto 13
elseif input fortune
    goto 33
elseif input fountain
    goto 45
endif

text You move into the market.
text A vendor immediately gets your attention.
text "Welcome fair lad! Are you new in town? Well I, the greatest antiquities dealer in all the realm, have something very special for you!
text And because you are new in town I'm willing to give a discount! Only 7 dubloons for this fine lamp!
text What do you say my new friend? Do you want to BUY it or RETURN?
input
if input buy
    goto 24
elseif input return
    goto 4
endif
if var money 7
    text I knew you'd like it! You are the proud owner a very rare item, indeed!
    scene src/end.txt
elseif var money 0
    text What do you think you're doing you beggar!
    text You have no money! Get out of here!
    goto 4
endif

text You move toward the fortune teller hoping to get a reading.
text "Welcome to my humble services, I am Madam Flick. Would you like a free READING or to RETURN?
input
if input reading
    text Yes, I see great financial fortune in your future!
    text You only have to go out and take it for yourself!
    text I also see something to do with water... very strange indeed.
    goto 4
elseif input return
    goto 4
endif

text You move toward the fountain in the center of the square.
text You look into the water and initially see your reflection but as you look closer you see the fountain is filled with dubloons!
text Would you TAKE some dubloons or RETURN to the village?
input
if input take
    text You decide to humbly take only 7 dubloons from the fountain.
    var money 7
    goto 4
elseif input return
    goto 4
endif
